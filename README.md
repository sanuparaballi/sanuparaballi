## 👋 About Me

I'm a PhD candidate researching how to make **NP-hard, non-convex optimization problems tractable** using evolutionary algorithms and surrogate models, and how to make the neural networks built on top of them **explainable enough to trust in production**. Before Syracuse, I spent 8+ years as a production engineer and delivery lead at Deloitte and Accenture, shipping APIs and enterprise platforms used by tens of thousands of people. That combination, research rigor plus shipping discipline, is what I bring to applied ML roles.

### Selected Results

| Problem | Approach | Result |
|---|---|---|
| NP-hard bilevel / combinatorial optimization | Surrogate-assisted co-evolutionary ES (decoupled search + evaluation) | **~96% compute reduction**, validated on Max-Cut |
| Distilling deep RL policies into interpretable rules | Hierarchical TSK Fuzzy Classifier System | **81.48% action-matching fidelity** (+21 pts over decision trees); 4-rule variant hit **97.8%** |
| Cascade popularity prediction on social networks | DFS-biased node2vec embeddings | Outperforms state-of-the-art **CasFlow** baseline |
| Enterprise API platform (S&P 500 client) | Architected + led delivery team | Sustained **~10M weekly requests** across ~35 sub-vendor platforms |
